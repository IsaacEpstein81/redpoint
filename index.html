Engagement Phases

A four-week configuration sprint gets Redpoint to launch readiness, then the subscription model takes over.

The launch plan is compact and operational: gather artifacts, configure the platform, review with stakeholders, finalize, and go live.

Week 1

Kickoff + Artifact Review

Collect Redpoint documents, review RAG materials, and align on the priority internal use cases for launch.

Week 2

Copilot Configuration

Complete knowledge ingestion, stand up the Organizational Copilot, and align response style to Redpoint terminology and standards.

Week 3

Roleplay + Control Tower Configuration

Configure AI roleplay workflows, stand up supervisor-facing views, and collect stakeholder feedback.

Week 4

Refinement + Launch Readiness

Finalize revisions, QA the configured experience, secure approval, and prepare for live deployment.

Phase 2

Ongoing Subscription

After launch, Redpoint remains on the configured platform with continued access, reasonable document and AI training updates, and the option to scope additional custom work separately later.
