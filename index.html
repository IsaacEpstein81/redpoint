Engagement Phases

A four-week configuration sprint gets Redpoint to launch readiness, then the subscription model takes over.

The launch plan is compact and operational: gather artifacts, configure the platform, review with stakeholders, finalize, and go live.

Week 1

Kickoff + Artifact Review

Collect Redpoint documents, review RAG materials, and align on the core staff and client-facing use cases.

Week 2

Copilot Configuration

Complete knowledge ingestion, set up the Organizational Copilot, and align response style to Redpoint terminology and standards.

Week 3

Client AI + Stakeholder Review

Configure naming, tone, and methodology framing for the client-facing experience, then collect feedback from stakeholders.

Week 4

Refinement + Launch Readiness

Finalize revisions, QA the configured experience, secure approval, and prepare for live deployment.

Phase 2

Ongoing Subscription

After launch, Redpoint remains on the configured OpenRecovery platform with continued access, reasonable document and AI training updates, and the option to scope any future custom work separately.
